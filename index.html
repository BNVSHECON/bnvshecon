About BNV SHE CON

Your trusted partner for comprehensive EHS management and sustainable business practices

BNV SHE CON is an EHS (Environment, Health & Safety) consulting firm founded by industry veteran Vijay Kumar BN, dedicated to enabling businesses achieve sustainable growth through robust safety systems, environmental compliance, and holistic EHS management.

We specialize in building and sustaining Management Systems for business sustainability across all functional groups — using a Top Down & Bottom-up approach on EHS domains, guided by the principles of the "Toyota Way in EHS Management."

Our mission is to help organizations establish a culture where "Safety First & Environment Must" is not just a slogan, but a lived practice — eliminating accidents, reducing carbon footprint, and building resilient, future-ready operations.
